About Me

Who I am and what drives my curiosity

Hi! I'm Prekshya, a deep learning research assistant who's passionate about understanding how artificial intelligence works. I'm in the early stages of my research journey, exploring the fascinating intersection of mathematics, computer science, and cognitive science that makes machine learning possible.

What excites me most about this field is not just the impressive capabilities of modern AI systems, but understanding the fundamental principles that make learning possible. I believe in learning by building - implementing algorithms from scratch, visualizing concepts, and sharing my discoveries along the way.

This website is my digital lab notebook, where I document my explorations, share my learnings, and hopefully inspire others who are on a similar journey. Whether I end up making groundbreaking discoveries or not, I'm committed to contributing to our collective understanding of artificial intelligence.

What I'm Working Towards

  • Building strong mathematical foundations for ML research
  • Understanding neural networks from first principles
  • Contributing to open source ML projects
  • Sharing knowledge through writing and teaching
  • Exploring applications of AI for social good
  • Connecting with the ML research community

If you're interested in my work, want to collaborate, or just want to chat about AI/ML, feel free to reach out! I'm always eager to connect with fellow learners and researchers.
